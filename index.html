from me to you

You are a magical! Yes, you!

You are experiencing life, made up of all nature’s elements - the air you breathe, the water you drink, the sun and moon that interchange your day are all part of your magic. There is an undeniable truth that when we separate ourselves from nature, we can feel off, so let’s get back to nature together. I believe in the magic in each of us and more importantly building the mindset that together we can change the way we think about, interact and live on this planet together.

Join me on my journey towards a more conscious life together, exploring the world, eating my weight in planet friendly foods and finding ways to build more magic in life.

With love,
Your fellow Tiny Earthling .x

Why I choose to live a conscious lifestyle?

A conscious lifestyle is living with awareness, kind curiosity and care for ourself, the people around us and the environment. As an avid traveler I want to see the planet alive, see forests breathing, oceans swaying, animals wild and free -- living with intention to see our planet thrive.
